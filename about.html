Who We Are

Abdiwahid & Associates Advocates, a premier law firm based in Mombasa, provides comprehensive legal services in various fields, including conveyance, civil and commercial litigation, industrial and labor relations, banking and securities, corporate and commercial law, mergers and acquisitions, succession, probate, and administration.

Our Clients

Our extensive client base includes leading banking and financial institutions, insurance companies, venture capital and institutional equity investors, industrial and commercial entities, professional firms, and international investment companies. We pride ourselves on delivering tailored legal solutions that align with the strategic goals and operational requirements of each client.

Our Location

Conveniently situated in the heart of Kizingo, our offices offer close proximity to the Mombasa Law Courts and major governmental and corporate offices. Equipped with state-of-the-art telecommunications infrastructure, we ensure seamless and efficient communication with our clients, locally and internationally.

Our Mission

To offer efficient and innovative legal solutions that enable our clients to achieve their goals.

Our Vision

To be a premier law firm renowned for quality legal services that add value to our clients.

Our Core Values

We are committed to excellence, innovation, and efficiency in all our legal services.
